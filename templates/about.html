About Titan Global FX

We are a US-based, SEC-compliant financial services firm dedicated to providing institutional-grade trading infrastructure to traders worldwide.

Our Story

Titan Global FX was founded in 2015 with a singular vision: to democratize institutional-grade trading by combining cutting-edge technology with uncompromising regulatory standards. Our founder, Jonathan R. Ashford, identified a critical gap in the market — retail traders were being underserved by platforms that lacked the speed, transparency, and reliability that institutions demand.

Today, Titan Global FX serves over 120,000 active traders across more than 150 countries, processing billions in monthly trading volume. We remain fiercely independent and committed to our core mission: providing fair, transparent, and lightning-fast execution to every trader, regardless of account size.

Our team of former institutional traders, technologists, and compliance experts work tirelessly to maintain the highest standards of security, performance, and regulatory adherence. We are proud to be a registered SEC Investment Adviser and FINRA member firm.
